Mission

We are responsible for over 60% of the tea produced in Kenya.From planting to packaging,every process is expertly managed to ensure we produce the original high-quality Kenyan teas the world has grown to love Through continouously improving our processes we strive to adhere to ethical standards for producing our tea throughout our value chain. We care about world and the people in it.Some of the 54 tea factory companies have expanded by setting up satellite factories in their neighbourhoods to accomodate the extra leaf. The satellite factories are 17 ,adding up to 71 the total number of tea factories owned by smallholder tea farmers.

Vision

Smallholder cultivation in Kenya commenced in the 1950's.In 1957,the first smallholder tea factory was setup in Ragati,Nyeri,Central Kenya,run through a management with multinational tea companies.In 1960 ,the special Crops Development Authority was established under the Ministry of Agricullture to oversee this crucial sector.
In 1964,the Kenya Tea Development Authority was established under the Agriculture Act (Cap318) Section 91,Legal Notice No 42,replacing the Special Crops Development Authority. The Authority took over management of small holder tea from multinational tea companies. In 1999 KTDA Order revoked through Legal Notice No 14.
In 2009 Kenya Tea Development Agency Ltd renamed to Kenya Tea Development Agency Holdings Ltd. Also the Kenya Tea Development Agency (KTDA Management Services Ltd ) was established
