    MATERIAL DETAIL

    All KTDA factories have acquired FDA manufacturer registration numbers to ascertain that the tea is manufactured following safe and health standards as required by the United States Department of Health and Human Services. The Standards ensure that all our factories maintain top quality manufacture process that minimize or get rid of contamination instances to assure our customers of safe and high quality tea. The certification also ensures our farmers adhere to the best agricultural practices for land use farm management and protection of the natural resources for sustainable tea farming,the certification has been adopted by all our facories.Our factories are geographically positioned in regions with favourable weather conditions for tea growth and unique features that characterise the sorroundings.
